# 1.6 End Session Flow

## Scope
- The rating page displayed to both client and companions after the session ends.
- Covers: rating structure, submission rules, and post-rating redirect.

### 1.6.1 Overview
*   This page appears automatically once the session has ended (see 1.5.1.5).
*   The UI layout is **similar for client and companion**, but the content and submission rules differ.
*   After submitting (or skipping, for client), the user is redirected to the **home screen**.

---

### 1.6.2 Client Rating

##### 1.6.2.1 Rating Structure
The client gives a **single overall rating** for the experience (not per companion). The rating page has three sections:

*   **1.6.2.1.1 Star Rating:** Out of **5 stars**.
*   **1.6.2.1.2 Auto-Select Tags:** Predefined tags for good/bad aspects of the experience (e.g., "Friendly", "Professional", "Late"). Tags are about the overall duo experience. *(Specific tags to be decided later.)*
*   **1.6.2.1.3 Text Feedback:** Optional free-text box for additional comments.

##### 1.6.2.2 Submission Rules
*   Rating is **optional** for the client. The client can skip and go directly to the home screen.

---

### 1.6.3 Companion Rating

##### 1.6.3.1 Rating Structure
**Both** Captain and Vice Captain rate the client **independently**. Each companion's rating page has three sections:

*   **1.6.3.1.1 Star Rating:** Out of **5 stars**.
*   **1.6.3.1.2 Auto-Select Tags:** Predefined tags for good/bad aspects of the client. *(Specific tags to be decided later.)*
*   **1.6.3.1.3 Text Feedback:** Optional free-text box for additional comments.

##### 1.6.3.2 Submission Rules
*   Rating is **mandatory** for companions. They cannot skip.

---

### 1.6.4 API
*   **[POST] /bookings/{id}/rating** — submits the rating (star rating, selected tags, text feedback). Used by both client and companions.

### 1.6.5 Post-Rating
*   After submission (or skip for client), both client and companions are redirected to the **home screen**.
