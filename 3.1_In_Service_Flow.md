# 3.1 In-Service Flow

## Scope
- This phase covers all interactions and operations while the companions are with the client.
- Begins after client matching and ends when the session is terminated.

### 3.1.1 Client Flow
#### 3.1.1.1 Instruction Screen
- The client will see a dedicated screen to send instructions to the companions.
- Specific instruction flows will be detailed in the `3.2 Command Flow` section.

#### 3.1.1.2 End Session Option
- The client will have an option to end the session at any time using an "End Session" button on the screen.
- Ending the session will trigger a confirmation prompt saying: "Are you sure? If yes, then the session will end."
- A modal will be used for confirmation to avoid accidental termination.

#### 3.1.1.3 Helpline Icon
- A helpline icon will be available on the screen.
- The client can use this to contact support for assistance during the session.
- The helpline will connect to the helpline server. Whether it connects to a live agent or an automated system will be decided later.
- The support team will first understand the issue and act as per the SOPs.
- The helpline will be available only during the service time when the service screen is active.

#### 3.1.1.4 Additional Instructions
- Other instructions that the client can send to the companions will be covered in the `3.2 Command Flow` section.
