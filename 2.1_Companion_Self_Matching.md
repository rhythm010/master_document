# 2.1 Companion Self-Matching

## Scope
- The end-to-end flow of companions preparing for and completing their self-matching (Duo activation) before the client arrives.
- Covers: shift-based notifications, travel to venue, arrival, QR/PIN matching between Captain and Vice Captain, and the transition to "Duo Activated" state.
- This is a **companion-only** flow. The client has no visibility or interaction during this phase.
- Begins after booking confirmation (1.2) and ends when the Duo is activated and ready for client matching (2.2).

### 2.1.1 Overview
* The Companion Self-Matching flow ensures both assigned companions (Captain + Vice Captain) physically meet at the venue, verify each other's identity, and activate as a Duo — all before the client arrives.
* **Important Distinction — Roster Time vs Booking Time:**
    * **Roster Time** = the start of the companion's **Shift** (e.g., 10:00 AM). A shift may contain 0 or more bookings.
    * **Booking Time** = the start of a specific **Booking** within a shift (e.g., 10:30 AM, 1:00 PM).
    * All pre-deployment notifications below reference **Roster Time** (shift start), not individual booking times. A companion reports once per shift, and the matching process happens once per shift start.

### 2.1.2 Pre-Arrival Notifications

#### 2.1.2.1 T-4h: Shift Reminder
* Notifications are sent to both companions with static information and guidelines:
    * Ensure 100% battery.
    * Stay hydrated.
    * Arrive at the designated venue on time.
* No confirmation is required at this stage.

#### 2.1.2.2 T-2h: Urgency Prompt
* The UI highlights **"Companion Match Pending"** with a backward timer to emphasize urgency.
* The Duo status remains **"Not Active."**
* No confirmation is required at this stage.

#### 2.1.2.3 T-30m: Location Confirmation
* A notification prompts companions to confirm their location.
* Companions must open the app and press the **"Confirm"** button to acknowledge the notification.
* This confirmation captures their GPS location and ensures they are en route to the venue.
* Failure to confirm activates the **Alternate Companion Readiness Flow**, and the admin is alerted.

### 2.1.3 Travel & Arrival at Venue

#### 2.1.3.1 Expected Arrival
* Companions are expected to arrive at the venue **30 minutes before the roster time**.
* The remaining 30 minutes serve as a grace period to ensure readiness.

#### 2.1.3.2 Reminder Notification
* A reminder notification is sent to companions as they approach the end of the grace period.
* This ensures they are aware of the deadline to be at the venue.

#### 2.1.3.3 Location Tracking
* Location tracking begins **45 minutes before the roster time** and ends once the companions are confirmed to be at the venue.
* The system continuously monitors their GPS location during this period to ensure they are en route.

#### 2.1.3.4 Navigation Assistance
* Companions use Google Maps location services to navigate to the venue.
* Additional details, such as nearby stores or landmarks, are provided to assist navigation.
* The system tracks their GPS location to ensure they are within the designated geo-fenced area.

### 2.1.4 Companion-to-Companion Matching (QR/PIN)

#### 2.1.4.1 QR Scan
* The **Captain** scans the QR code displayed by the **Vice Captain** to complete the match.
* If the QR code scan fails:
    * The system allows up to **two attempts** to scan the QR code.
    * After two failed attempts, a **manual PIN code** option becomes visible.
    * The manual PIN code is a **static fallback code** generated specifically for that booking.

#### 2.1.4.2 Successful Match
* Upon successful matching:
    * The Duo status changes to **"Activated"** (`duo_status`: `PENDING` → `MATCHED` → `ACTIVATED`).
    * Both companions receive a confirmation notification.

#### 2.1.4.3 Audit Logging
* The system logs the matching event for audit purposes, including:
    * Timestamp of the match.
    * GPS location of the companions.
    * Device ID used for scanning.
    * Companion roles (Captain / Vice Captain).
    * Method used (`QR_SCAN` or `MANUAL_PIN`).

#### 2.1.4.4 Failure Handling
* If both the QR code and manual PIN code options fail, the system notifies the admin.
* The admin must manually match the companions to complete the process.
* **Auto-Cancellation Deadline (T-20m):** If the companions have not successfully matched by **20 minutes before booking start**, the booking is **automatically cancelled** by the system.
    * The client is notified, refunded, and freed to rebook.
    * Both companions (or the missing companion) receive a violation record.
    * Hot Swap (reassigning to another Duo) is **not supported** in this version.

### 2.1.5 Post-Matching (Duo Activated → Awaiting Client)

#### 2.1.5.1 Companion Screen
* Once the Duo is activated, the companions' screens display booking details, including:
    * Booking timing.
    * Meeting location (if set by client).
    * Client nickname.
* The **"Notes"** section is included but cannot be edited by the companions. It may be empty if no additional instructions are provided.

#### 2.1.5.2 Client Notification Trigger
* The system notifies the client that the companions are ready and waiting to welcome them.
* The client can view the companions' real-time location on the app.
* If the client does not respond, the escalation ladder begins (see 2.2.2.1).

#### 2.1.5.3 Handoff to Client Matching
* Once the Duo is activated and the client has been notified, the flow transitions to **2.2 Client Matching & Deployment**.
* Companions remain at the venue and await the client's arrival and instructions.

---

### 2.1.6 Key Rules & Constraints
| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Roster-based, not booking-based** | Notifications reference Roster Time (shift start). Companions report once per shift. |
| **General Roster** | Companions are not locked to a specific venue. They travel to the assigned venue from a general pool. |
| **T-20m Auto-Cancel** | If companions haven't matched by 20 minutes before booking start → automatic cancellation, client refunded, violation issued. |
| **No Hot Swap** | Reassigning to another Duo mid-flow is not supported in this version. |
| **QR → PIN → Admin** | Three-tier fallback: QR scan (2 attempts) → manual PIN → admin manual match. |
| **GPS Always On** | Companions must have GPS enabled at all times as per job role requirements. |
